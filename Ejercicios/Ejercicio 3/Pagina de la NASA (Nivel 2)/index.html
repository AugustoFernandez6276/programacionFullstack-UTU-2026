How Open Data Captured 3I/ATLAS

Comet 3I/ATLAS will soon leave our solar system forever, but the data collected about it will remain available in NASA’s public archives. More than a dozen NASA missions observed the comet, which is only the third identified object to visit our solar system from interstellar space.
